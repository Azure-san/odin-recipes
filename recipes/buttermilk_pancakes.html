Buttermilk Pancakes

These are the best buttermilk pancakes. They are light and fluffy with a touch of maple flavor.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Buttermilk
  • Eggs
  • Maple syrup
  • Baking powder
  1. Whisk and everything together in a large mixing bowl
  2. Whisk buttermilk and the others in another bowl
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, cook until browned on the other side
