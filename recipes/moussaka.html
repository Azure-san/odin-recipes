Moussaka

This Moussaka is made of layers of sliced eggplants baked in a ground beef sauce

This dish is also smothered in a creamy white bechamel sauce. The Moussaka is a classic Greek dish

  • Eggplants
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  1. Prepare the eggplants
  2. Fry the ground beef and all that stuff
  3. Butter and flour to make the cream
  4. Bake the Moussaka
